The brief

Everything the article didn't tell you

Four panels of AI-powered analysis, delivered in seconds — right inside your browser.

Source intelligence — Who owns it, who funds it, and where it sits on the political spectrum.

Key claims — The article's main assertions, rated as supported, disputed, or needing context.

Missing context — Facts, history, and counterarguments the article conveniently left out.

Other perspectives — How different outlets would cover the same story differently.

Built for skeptics

What makes ContextBot different

01
Works on any page

NYT, Fox News, Substack, local blogs — ContextBot works anywhere you read articles. Not just a curated list of approved sources.

02
Real-time AI analysis

Unlike static bias databases, ContextBot analyzes the specific article you're reading — its claims, its framing, its omissions — in real time.

03
No agenda

We don't sell advertising. We don't have a political bent. We don't profit from outrage. We just help you read smarter.

04
Stays out of the way

ContextBot only appears when you ask for it. No banners, no popups, no interruptions. Your reading experience stays yours.
